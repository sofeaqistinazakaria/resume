My Lovely Family


I am sure that family holds a special place in everyone’s life. I love my family members and they have given me so much love and affection and have made me who I am today. Here is my short description aboy my family.

In my family, basically we are nine members altogether that include my father, mother, five big sisters, a brother and me. We all live together in our own house until one by one of my siblings went to study and work away from Kelantan. My father, Zakaria Bin Talib is a retired worker meanwhile my mother, Rakiah Binti Mustapa is a housewife who take care of my family. My parents always encourages us to be disciplined and good in studies.

Basically, I have 7 siblings and I am the youngest one. I am lucky because my sisters always try to help me in my studies since primary school. But I can't deny that it is quite hard to grow alone especially when your oldest siblings already make their own ways in life but they always make sure that I would not feel left out. We help and support each other during the good as well as the bad times. Once in a while, we will go for a vacation together and have a lot of fun together.

Growing up surrounded by such loving and affectionate family is like a dream. Ours is a close knit family and that is what I love the most about it. The credit of keeping the family together and maintaining a strong bond certainly goes to my parents who have given such good values to us. My family is my life and thus it is most important to me than any other thing.
